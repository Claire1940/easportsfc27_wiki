<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Custom Tactics Setup">

Configuring your custom tactics correctly before entering a match will prevent your defense from being exposed by fast wingers. Follow these steps to set up the ultimate blueprint.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Set Defensive Style">
    Choose "Balanced" with a width of 45 and a depth of 55. This keeps your three central defenders compact while preventing long balls over the top.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Configure Build-up Play">
    Set build-up play to "Balanced" and chance creation to "Direct Passing." This encourages your strikers to make runs into channels the moment you win possession.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Assign Player Instructions">
    Ensure your defensive midfielder is set to "Cut Passing Lanes" and "Stay Back While Attacking" to protect the back three during transitions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Optimize Corner and Free Kick Settings">
    Set players in the box to 3 bars. This keeps enough physical presence forward without leaving your defense vulnerable to rapid counter-attacks.
  </Step>
</Steps>
