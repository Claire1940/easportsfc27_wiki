A core pillar of the **ea sports fc 27 new engine** is the rumored implementation of Trae Young technology. Named after pioneering tracking methodologies, this system represents a departure from traditional studio-based motion capture. Instead of placing actors in suits inside a studio, this technology extracts volumetric data directly from real-world professional matches.

Sprinting profiles, sudden changes of direction, jumping heights, and even post-possession recovery animations are translated directly into the game.
