<Section icon={Settings} title="Step-by-Step PC Optimization Guide">

Once you have confirmed your hardware meets the **ea sports fc 27 system requirements**, you should perform a clean setup to maximize frame rates. PC gaming offers flexibility, but it also requires manual tuning to avoid background software interference.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Update Graphics Drivers">
    Visit the official Nvidia, AMD, or Intel websites to download the latest game-ready drivers. These updates often include specific optimizations for the Frostbite Engine used in FC 27.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Configure Windows Game Mode">
    Enable "Game Mode" in Windows 10/11 settings. This prevents Windows Update from installing drivers in the background and allocates more GPU resources to the active game window.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Set Power Plan to High Performance">
    Navigate to the Control Panel > Power Options and select "High Performance." This ensures your CPU doesn't throttle its clock speed to save energy during intense matches.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Adjust In-Game Refresh Rate">
    Match the in-game refresh rate to your monitor's native capability (e.g., 60Hz, 144Hz). Using an uncapped frame rate can lead to screen tearing if G-Sync or FreeSync is not active.
  </Step>
</Steps>
